Paragliding Guide

What to Bring for Paragliding in Jardín

A little preparation can make your day feel easier and more comfortable. Here are simple things to bring for a paragliding day in the mountains around Jardín.

View Checklist Back to Paragliding

Simple Essentials

Basic Checklist

  • Closed-toe shoes
  • Comfortable clothes
  • Light jacket or extra layer
  • Sunscreen
  • Water
  • Sunglasses if appropriate for the day

Clothing

What to Wear

Comfortable clothes are usually the best choice. Since mountain conditions can change, simple layers tend to work better than heavy clothing.

Closed-toe shoes are a smart option for outdoor activities and are usually a better fit than sandals or anything loose.

Good Clothing Choices

  • T-shirt or breathable top
  • Comfortable pants or shorts
  • Light jacket
  • Secure shoes with grip

Mountain Conditions

Why Layers Help

Weather in mountain areas can shift during the day. Even when the town feels warm, conditions around outdoor activity areas may feel a little different.

A light extra layer makes it easier to stay comfortable without overpacking.

Small Extras That Help

  • Water for before or after the activity
  • Sunscreen for daytime conditions
  • A small bag for basics
  • Minimal loose items

Keep It Simple

What Not to Overdo

You usually do not need to bring too much. The goal is to stay comfortable, dress simply, and avoid carrying unnecessary items.

A light, practical setup often works best for a day built around one outdoor activity.
