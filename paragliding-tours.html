Planning Tips

What to Bring

Comfortable clothing, secure shoes, and light layers are a smart choice for mountain activities. Weather can shift during the day, so it helps to dress simply and be prepared for changing conditions.

If you are planning a full day outdoors, water and sunscreen are also good basics to bring along.

Good to Bring

  • Closed-toe shoes
  • Comfortable clothes
  • Light jacket or layer
  • Sunscreen
  • Water
