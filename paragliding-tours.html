Planning Tips

What to Bring

Comfortable clothing, secure shoes, and light layers are a smart choice for mountain activities. Weather can shift during the day, so it helps to dress simply and be prepared for changing conditions.

If you are planning a full day outdoors, water and sunscreen are also good basics to bring along.

Good to Bring

  • Closed-toe shoes
  • Comfortable clothes
  • Light jacket or layer
  • Sunscreen
  • Water

Safety

Safety and Conditions

Paragliding experiences depend on weather and wind conditions, so flight timing can vary from day to day. Visitors should expect local operators to make decisions based on conditions at the launch area.

If you are considering a flight, it is a good idea to ask questions in advance about timing, comfort level, and any basic participation guidelines.
