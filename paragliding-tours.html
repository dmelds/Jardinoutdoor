Experience Overview

What It’s Like

Paragliding in Jardín offers wide mountain views, fresh air, and a unique way to see the landscape around town. Many visitors choose tandem flights, which means flying with an experienced pilot.

For travelers looking for a standout activity, this can become one of the most memorable parts of the trip.

Scenic Views

Take in valleys, hillsides, and the green mountain landscape from above.

Tandem Experience

A good option for first-time visitors who want a guided experience.

Memorable Adventure

A signature outdoor activity for travelers who want something exciting.

Planning Tips

Before You Go

Weather can change in the mountains, so flexible planning helps. Comfortable clothes, secure shoes, and a light layer are usually a smart choice for the day.

Many travelers combine paragliding with coffee, a walk through town, or another outdoor activity later in the day.

Good to Bring

  • Closed-toe shoes
  • Comfortable clothes
  • Light jacket
  • Sunscreen
  • Water
